# On the Theory of Elliptic Functions

English translation of the 1877 paper "Zur Theorie der elliptischen Functionen" by Ferdinand Georg Frobenius and Ludwig Stickelberger.

## About

This repository contains an English translation of a short but influential paper from the *Journal für die reine und angewandte Mathematik* (Crelle's Journal), Vol. 83, pp. 175-179 (1877).

The paper establishes key determinant formulas for elliptic functions, connecting results of Hermite and Kiepert through an elegant limiting process. The main results include:

1. A general limiting formula for determinants of analytic functions (Equation 1)
2. Hermite's determinant formula for the Weierstrass ℘-function (Equation 3)
3. Kiepert's determinant for derivatives of ℘ (Equation 4)
